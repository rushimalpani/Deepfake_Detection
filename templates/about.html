Welcome to Deepfake Detector

Our Mission

To create a safer digital environment by empowering individuals, organizations, and governments with innovative deepfake detection tools. We aim to promote media transparency and reduce the harmful impact of manipulated content.

Features

  • Accurate detection of manipulated videos using advanced AI models.
  • Identification of audio deepfakes through voice pattern analysis.
  • Recognition of text-based manipulations using natural language processing.
  • User-friendly interface for seamless media analysis.
